Hive Rules

Be respectful, this includes being polite and civil to other members, not using offensive language or making personal attacks, and refraining from discrimination or harassment.
Stay on topic, keep your posts relevant to the discussion at hand and avoid going off-topic.
No spamming, do not post the same message or advertisement repeatedly or send unsolicited private messages.
No trolling, do not post messages intended to provoke or annoy other members.
No illegal content, do not post or share any illegal or copyrighted content.
No plagiarism, do not copy and paste content from other sources without proper attribution.
No impersonation, do not pretend to be someone else or use multiple accounts to deceive others.
No posting of inappropriate content: Users may be prohibited from posting content that is pornographic, violent, or otherwise inappropriate for the forum's audience.
No minor is allowed, age restrictions is important to ensure that only users of 18 years and above can participate in the community.
